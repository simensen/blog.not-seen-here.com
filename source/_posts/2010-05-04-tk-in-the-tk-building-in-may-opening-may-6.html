THE CORRIDOR GALLERY (TK BUILDING) / THURSDAY MAY 6 / 6–9PM

THE SHOW: I’m excited to announce the opening of my newest show at The Corridor Gallery. The opening begins at 6pm on Thursday, part of the Downtown First Thursday Artwalk. The show will run until early June. The Corridor Gallery is located in the TK Building at Third Avenue and Washington Street in Pioneer Square.

THE WORK: My paintings are an exercise in abstraction. They are studies in shapes, colors, layers and their ability to provoke unexpected thoughts and reactions. For the first time, I’ll be showing two of my newest, limited edition prints. (see attached image)

DO GOOD: 10% of all sales from this show will be donated to Seattle Children’s Hospital.
